Fire Walk
With Us

Fire Walk With Us literally arose from the ashes. In 2015 their practice space burned down and all equipment was lost in the fire. The search for a new home and rebuilding the band postponed the release of their second full-length album.

The album was recorded by Quintijn Verhoef at Studio Independent Recordings and was mixed by the band themselves and mastered by Robbe K. Its 5 tracks encompass the expansiveness of postrock and the hard-hitting tones of metal in equal measure.

Fire indeed walked with them, but it didn't burn them down. An albums worth of new material has already been written and will see the light of day sooner than later.
