Fire Walk
With Us

Fire Walk With Us literally arose from the ashes. In 2015 their rehearsal studio burned down and all equipment was lost in the fire. The search for a new home and rebuilding the band postponed the release of their second full-length album.

This fall it will finally see the light of day. Its five tracks encompass the expansiveness of postrock and the hard-hitting tones of metal in equal measure. The album was recorded by Quintijn Verhoef at Studio Independent Recordings, mixed by the band themselves and mastered by Robbe K. at Audiovisualz.

Fire indeed walked with them, but it didn't burn them down. Stay tuned!
